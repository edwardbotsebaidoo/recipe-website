Lasagna

Ingredients needed:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

    Prepare Noodles: Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    Cook Meat: Brown ground meat with diced onions and minced garlic in olive oil.
    Layer Ingredients: In a baking dish, layer cooked noodles, meat mixture, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
    Repeat Layers: Repeat layering until ingredients are used up, finishing with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
    Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
    Serve: Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious lasagna.
